# Lean exercises for an Algebra lecture

The lecture *Algebra and Number Theory* (original name in German: *Algebra und Zahlentheorie*) was an introductory lecture to abstract algebra, polynomial rings and Galois theory, taught by Annette Huber-Klawitter at the University of Freiburg during the Winter Semester of 2021/2022. I was a teaching assistant for this lecture and one of my duties was to develop weekly exercise sheets. Since this topic is particularly suitable to be formalized in Lean, I proposed several **Lean exercises** in these exercise sheets. The homepage for the lecture is available at [https://home.mathematik.uni-freiburg.de/arithgeom/lehre/ws21/azt](https://home.mathematik.uni-freiburg.de/arithgeom/lehre/ws21/azt).

- The exercise sheets containing Lean exercises are sheets 1, 2, 3, 4, and 6. I provided hints to each of them, and for sheets 1, 2 and 3 I also provided more detailed hints and full solutions. The original files (written in German and for Lean 3) are available in the [original-files](original-files/) folder.
- English versions of the relevant exercises and solutions written for Lean 4 are avaialbe in the remaining folders.
